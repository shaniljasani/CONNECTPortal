CONNECT Helpdesk Support Form

To get help on any issues you have, please complete the form below.

Provide as much detail as you can. We would like to know:

  1. Device Type
  2. What application or program you are having issues with
  3. What aspect of the application is causing the issue (ie “I cannot hear anything in the Zoom”)
  4. Screenshots are great, if you are able to send one with your ticket
  5. If you are submitting a ticket on the behalf of someone else, please provide the contact information for the person experiencing issues

If you do not know your ID Number, please enter 0000 and mention this in the description

After submitting a ticket you will receive a response email from the helpdesk. If you want to update your ticket with any additional information, please respond to the email from the support team.
