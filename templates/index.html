About CONNECT

Global Encounters and Aga Khan Youth and Sports Boards are partnering to offer a virtual camp for the youth of the global Jamat. CONNECT: a journey of exploration, learning and creativity is designed to strengthen connections amongst our global youth. The camp will offer opportunities to engage in cultural exchange, learn new skills, explore the AKDN, collaborate on creative projects, make new friends, dialogue about current issues, and build for the future. The theme for the upcoming camp is climate change and social action. Participants will have a unique opportunity to build skills and collaborate on creative projects through workshops.
